Relive the classics

Create new experiences for the Mario Party 64 trio.

Play on real hardware

PartyPlanner64 exports ROMs that will work on your N64. All you need is a flash cartridge and an Expansion Pak.

Share your boards

Boards you create can be exported and shared with others. No need to create a ROM patch.
